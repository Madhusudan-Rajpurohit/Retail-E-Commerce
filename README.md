1. Retail Customer Segmentation using RFM (LATEST)
Key Findings:

Potential Loyalists generate 29% of total revenue (largest revenue segment)

Champions contribute 27.7% revenue from 21.8% customers

Top 2 segments account for 56.7% of overall revenue

One-Time Buyers represent 17.5% customers but only 12.4% revenue

Revenue moderately concentrated in high-value segments

Strategic Impact:

Retention priority: Protect Champions to reduce revenue risk

Growth opportunity: Convert Potential Loyalists into Champions

Reactivation strategy for At-Risk customers

Optimize marketing spend on low-value segments

Business Value Delivered:

Identified high-revenue customer clusters

Quantified revenue concentration risk

Provided actionable retention and growth strategy

Built executive-ready visualizations & insights
